USA heading to the Olympics

Female athletes to look out for in their Olympic deput:

Sophie Smith

At 23, Sophie Smith, a current member of the Portland Thorns, is making her Olympic debut this year. She is a leading goal scorer in the NWSL and will bring a lot offensively to this USA team.

Sha'Carri Richardson

At 24, Sha’Carri Richardson is on her redemption tour! After being ousted in the 2020 Olympics, she is getting her chance to compete at the highest level and we are excited to see how she does!

Sara Hughes

At 29, Sara Hughes is partnering with Kelly Cheng in her Olympic debut on her road to win their first Women’s Sand Volleyball Gold Medal!

Katharine Berkoff

At 23, Katharine Berkoff, daughter of Olympic swimmer David Berkoff, will be making her Olympic debut in her fight to win her first medal!

Hezly Rivera

At just 16, Hezly Rivera will be making her debut as the youngest Olympian on Team USA.

Follow them on their Olympic journey!
